Empowering black men to take control of their health

Our NGO provides a range of services and resources to support black men in their fight against cancer.

Education and awareness

We believe that knowledge is power. Through our educational programs, we aim to raise awareness about prostate cancer among black men.

Support groups and counselling

Our trained professionals provide a safe and supportive environment for individuals to share their experiences and find solace when needed.

Our voluteers

Our support groups and counselling services are provided by volunteers.
